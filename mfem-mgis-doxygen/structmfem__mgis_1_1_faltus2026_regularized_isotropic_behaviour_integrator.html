Detailed Description

template<Hypothesis H>
struct mfem_mgis::Faltus2026RegularizedIsotropicBehaviourIntegrator< H >

a behaviour integrator which enhances a standard finite strain behaviour with the regularization proposed by Faltus et al.

This regularization adds a term penalizing the difference between the deformation gradient \(\underline{F}\) and its value \(\bar{\underline{F}}\) at the centroid of the element:

\[ W\left(\underline{F}, \bar{\underline{F}}\right) = \alpha\,\left(\underline{F}-\bar{\underline{F}}\right)\,\colon\, \left(\underline{F}-\bar{\underline{F}}\right) \]
