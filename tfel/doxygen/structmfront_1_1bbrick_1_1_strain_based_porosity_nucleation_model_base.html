Detailed Description

A base class for nucleation models of the form:

\[ \dfrac{\mathrm{d}f}{\mathrm{d}t}=An(p)\,\dfrac{\mathrm{d}p}{\mathrm{d}t} \]

where:

  • \(f\) is the nucleated porosity.
  • \(p\) is the total equivalent plastic strain.
