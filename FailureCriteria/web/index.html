The FailureCriteria project

Jérémy Hure, Thomas Helfer

03/11/2026

  • 1 Available criteria
    • 1.1 Brittle failure
    • 1.2 Ductile failure
  • References

The project FailureCriteria provides MFront implementations of various criteria to estimate the risk of brittle and ductile failure. Those implementations can be used in uncoupled analyses, in the post-processing of the computations.

1 Available criteria

1.1 Brittle failure

  • Beremin et al. [1],
  • Ruggieri and Dodds [2],
  • Forget, Marini and Vincent [3].

1.2 Ductile failure

  • Freudenthal [4],
  • Cockcroft and Latham [5],
  • Rice and Tracey [6],
  • Hancock and McKenzie [7],
  • Johnson And Cook [8],
  • Huang [9],
  • Bai and Wierzbicki [10].
